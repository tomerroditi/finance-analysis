⚡
Amount Sign Convention (everywhere): Negative = money out (expenses, investment deposits). Positive = money in (salary, refunds, withdrawals). Applies across all tables, services, and calculations.
⚡
CC Deduplication — the critical pattern: Bank CC bill payment and itemized CC purchases overlap. Aggregate KPIs (income, net worth) → bank view, exclude CC source. Category breakdowns (pie charts, budgets) → itemized view, exclude "Credit Cards" category.
⚡
Prior Wealth bridges the gap: prior_wealth = entered_balance − sum(transactions). Injected as synthetic "Prior Wealth" tagged rows. Investment prior wealth lives in bank balance (money originally came from banks).
⚡
Investment Balance Resolution: Snapshot-first (manual > calculated > scraped), then transaction-sum fallback (−sum(all amounts)). Closing creates a 0-balance snapshot. Fixed-rate investments auto-generate daily-compounded snapshots.
⚡
Non-expense overrides: Investments, Liabilities, Income categories, Credit Cards excluded from expenses — except negative Liabilities (debt payments), which override back as real money outflows.
